# Meme And Folk Relic Objects

## Taxonomy Scope
This file groups meme and folk relic objects within the broader internet-folk style system.

## Main Families
- reaction images, rage faces, meme templates, repost watermarks, impact captions, ASCII faces, YTP debris, deep-fried shards
- each family can appear as literal UI furniture, floating collage matter, iconographic relic, or symbolic shorthand

## Era Range
- old web relics
- Y2K and MySpace customization culture
- early social-web residue
- forum and gallery communities
- meme and repost archaeology

## Aesthetic Roles
- functional object: something the page or platform actually uses
- decorative object: something added for personality or clutter
- identity object: something that signals taste, mood, status, or affiliation
- relic object: something that shows age, damage, dead links, or prior circulation

## Prompt Language
meme and folk relic objects, internet object family, archive-native furniture, legacy web ecology, social-interface debris, digital folk relics

## Taxonomy Seeds
1. build a composition only from meme and folk relic objects and let the subject emerge from the clutter
2. use meme and folk relic objects as floating iconographic fragments around a portrait or shrine page
3. arrange meme and folk relic objects into a dense flat-lay of internet life-support machinery
4. depict a dead or abandoned page where meme and folk relic objects remain as the surviving bones
5. treat meme and folk relic objects like archaeological finds from an extinct social platform
