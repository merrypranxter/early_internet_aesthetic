# Avatar Identity Performance

Internet-folk style often treats identity as an assembled surface: icon, username, signature, away message,
badge stack, profile song, top-friends order, and reaction-image taste all become pieces of the self.

## Visual consequences
- repeated face/icon imagery
- decorative handles and signature banners
- sentimental or ironic objects orbiting the avatar
- identity conveyed through customization more than physical realism

## Useful prompt phrases
avatar shrine, decorated identity, icon-stack selfhood, badge constellation, profile-song aura, handle ornament, customized self-surface
