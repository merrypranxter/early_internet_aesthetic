# WHY_IT_WORKS

This aesthetic works because it turns technological limitation, social remixing, and decorative excess into image language.

It hits several things at once:
- nostalgia
- humor
- community trace
- visible mediation
- emotional contradiction
- archive desire
- low-level material weirdness

In prompt systems, it is especially useful because it gives models many concrete handles:
- named objects
- known interfaces
- distinct damage behaviors
- strong palette families
- compositional density
- emotionally legible contradictions
