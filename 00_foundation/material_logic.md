# MATERIAL_LOGIC

## Digital matter in this style behaves like:
- cheap plastic
- soft phosphor glow
- compressed sand
- glitter glue
- browser chrome
- reflective acrylic
- dusty screenshots
- sticky desktop residue

## Common material states
- overcompressed
- semi-transparent
- browser-framed
- glittered
- screen-lit
- datamoshed
- cropped and recopied
- artifacted
- holographic
- washed by weak monitor light

## Important note
Do not reduce the repo to “vintage computer.” The material logic is not hardware fetishism. It is interface residue plus social transit.
