# EMOTIONAL_TONE

## Primary emotional bands

### Earnest homemade enthusiasm
The old-web energy of someone teaching themselves HTML and absolutely going for it.

### Decorative oversharing
MySpace energy: feelings externalized as glitter, layout, color, song, badges, and profile text.

### Absurd communal laughter
Meme energy: shared jokes, repeated templates, ugly-funny image construction.

### Archive melancholy
Dead forums, obsolete UI, deleted usernames, abandoned pages, lost links, ancient screenshots.

### Synthetic optimism
Y2K chrome, glossy gradients, Frutiger Aero softness, media-player futurism, fake-calm future vibes.

### Reverence for digital trash
Treating broken interfaces, bad crops, old popups, and error icons like relics.

## Tone balancing notes

The style gets stronger when it contains a contradiction:
- cute + damaged
- funny + sad
- holy + stupid
- glossy + broken
- crowded + intimate
- ugly + beloved
