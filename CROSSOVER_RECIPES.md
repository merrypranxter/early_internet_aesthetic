# CROSSOVER_RECIPES

## internet_folk x glitchcore
Keep browser/UI matter, compression damage, and screenshot lineage visible. Do not let it become generic cyberpunk.

## internet_folk x vaporwave
Use gradient cathedrals, dead malls, marble busts, and corporate afterlife space, but keep repost scarring or interface fossils in frame.

## internet_folk x emo/scene
Anchor with profile-page selfhood, glitter names, webcam flash, top-friends drama, decorative black/pink UI chaos.

## internet_folk x hyperpop
Push sticker clutter, candy LEDs, emoji gloss, dopamine overload, and ironic joy. Preserve browser remnants and low-res sincerity.

## internet_folk x weirdcore/dreamcore
Use nostalgic halls, cursed UI fragments, old-web signage, impossible page layouts, emotional liminality. Avoid pure horror unless requested.

## internet_folk x op art
Turn browser frames, buttons, counters, and comment-box rhythms into repeating optical systems. Keep the internet objects legible.

## internet_folk x corporate-decay
Emphasize abandoned lobbies, fake calm branding, mallsoft ruins, dead logos, fluorescent utopian residue.

## internet_folk x sacred/occult
Use digital shrines, 404 reliquaries, GIF basilicas, pop-up monstrances, sainted meme icons, but maintain screen matter and absurdity.
