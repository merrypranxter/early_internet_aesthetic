## Folder purposes

### 00_foundation
Definitions, theory, emotional logic, and practical operating principles.

### 01_eras
Chronological splits: old web, Y2K, MySpace, early social, deep internet, dead-web nostalgia.

### 02_core_branches
Primary style families you can use as standalone engines.

### 03_visual_elements
Type, layouts, browser fragments, glitter, buttons, icons, stickers, overlays.

### 04_materials_and_textures
Compression, CRT, webcam flash, browser residue, repost scarring, holographic plastic, UI matter.

### 05_motifs_and_objects
Concrete symbols and artifacts: guestbooks, counters, AIM emoticons, popups, rage faces, etc.

### 06_composition_and_behavior
Clutter systems, recursion, screenshot palimpsests, irony mechanics, collective authorship.

### 07_palettes
Palette families for fast swaps and modular prompt writing.

### 08_prompt_modules
Ready-made prompt modules and templates for subjects, scenes, collage, UI shrines, motion.

### 09_style_merges
Recipes for combining internet_folk_style with other aesthetics.

### 10_reference_lists
Large vocab sets, tag dumps, and search-friendly reference banks.

### 99_appendix
Source framing, naming rules, lineage, and future expansion notes.
