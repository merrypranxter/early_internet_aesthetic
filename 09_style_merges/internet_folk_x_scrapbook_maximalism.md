# Internet Folk X Scrapbook Maximalism

## Merge Thesis
This merge combines internet-folk vernacular with handmade cutout logic, glitter pens translated into PNGs, memory-hoard density, layered keepsake energy.

## Keep From Internet Folk
- visible repost lineage
- screenshot / UI residue
- collective, handmade, or customized mess
- irony and sincerity coexisting in the same frame

## Add From Secondary Style
- handmade cutout logic, glitter pens translated into PNGs, memory-hoard density, layered keepsake energy

## Watch Outs
Do not let the secondary style sterilize the vernacular weirdness. Keep browser debris, cheap texture, or user-authored clutter alive.

## Prompt Formula
subject + internet-folk clutter + handmade cutout logic, glitter pens translated into PNGs, memory-hoard density, layered keepsake energy + platform residue + visible emotional customization + anti-corporate mess-maximalism

## Example Snippet
A composition full of handmade cutout logic, glitter pens translated into PNGs, memory-hoard density, layered keepsake energy, but still scarred by repost life: window frames, cropped screenshots, low-res remnants,
status-line fragments, and anonymous folk texture.
