# Internet Folk X Analog Damage

## Merge Thesis
This merge combines internet-folk vernacular with scan artifacts, photocopy grime, CRT bleed, printed forum ghosts, thermal-receipt sadness, archive ruin texture.

## Keep From Internet Folk
- visible repost lineage
- screenshot / UI residue
- collective, handmade, or customized mess
- irony and sincerity coexisting in the same frame

## Add From Secondary Style
- scan artifacts, photocopy grime, CRT bleed, printed forum ghosts, thermal-receipt sadness, archive ruin texture

## Watch Outs
Do not let the secondary style sterilize the vernacular weirdness. Keep browser debris, cheap texture, or user-authored clutter alive.
